## E2 pilot 自动摘要

下表是 `strategy - FIFO` 的 seed 级平均配对差异及其 95% percentile bootstrap CI；
负值代表等待时间降低，正值代表恶化。

| prediction error | metric | predicted SJF mean delta | 95% CI |
|---|---|---:|---:|
| exact | P95 wait | -37.64 | [-45.70, -30.13] |
| exact | P99 wait | +25.39 | [+3.39, +52.26] |
| exact | long-class P99 wait | +236.26 | [+163.66, +323.59] |
| low | P95 wait | -36.89 | [-45.64, -29.06] |
| low | P99 wait | +30.66 | [+7.31, +63.40] |
| low | long-class P99 wait | +274.46 | [+190.96, +374.16] |
| high | P95 wait | -31.15 | [-38.18, -24.70] |
| high | P99 wait | +52.15 | [+24.35, +85.34] |
| high | long-class P99 wait | +295.30 | [+209.51, +396.75] |

预测器 sanity check：三档条件的平均 MAE 为 0.00/0.65/1.37，平均 Spearman
相关为 1.00/0.94/0.81。误差增加时 predicted SJF 的 P95 改善缩小，P99 与长任务
尾部伤害扩大；oracle 仅用于诊断，不是可部署策略。
